DELIVERABLE CONTAINING PERSONAS FOR THE GROUP PROJECT

INTRO

This deliverable page presents a summary on the personas that have been created for the group project. For the group project, it was initially not clear what the intended target audience was. Personas have been created in the past of the Grip on Sound project, but it was not clear to me what these personas were based on.

For defining the target audience further I set up a research question myself:

  • How can I clarify my intended target audience using accessible and available tools?

RESEARCH METHOD

By making use of literature study, I quickly stumbled across a YouTube video which explained how to define your target audience in three easy steps using ChatGPT.

EXECUTION

The YouTube video allows you to define your target audience with the help of personas in just three steps (Mike Costanzo, 2023). These steps were as follows:

  • My product is an app for physical education teachers who might not be aware of suffering potential hearing damage during loud gym lessons. Write a user persona.
  • Create a user persona for the opposite gender.
  • Where can I target these people online?
