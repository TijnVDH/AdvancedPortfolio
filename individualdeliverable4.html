CONTEXT

During my studies at Fontys ICT, I have programmed mainly in JavaScript, but I have never before worked with a front-end framework. This is the reason why I wanted to research whether the individual project is the right place to apply learning a front-end framework.

While I was working on my third-year internship, I noticed that all of the company's web applications are built with the help of a front-end framework. Hence why I have gotten interest in learning a front-end framework. During, and after my internship I have not gotten the chance to start learning, but this semester seems like a right time.
