FRONT-END FRAMEWORK RESEARCH

REPORT CONTAINING THE RESEARCH THAT HAS BEEN DONE REGARDING FRONT-END FRAMEWORKS.

INTRO

This deliverable page presents a summary of the front-end framework research that has been done. The process of approach, and the main results and conclusions will be discussed. At the bottom of the page you can find a button which directs towards the full research document. It is suggested to read through the summary first, after which the research document can be used as the official proof.

CONTEXT

During my studies at Fontys ICT, I have programmed mainly in JavaScript, but I have never before worked with a front-end framework. This is the reason why I wanted to research whether the individual project is the right place to apply learning a front-end framework.

While I was working on my third-year internship, I noticed that all of the company's web applications are built with the help of a front-end framework. Hence why I have gotten interest in learning a front-end framework. During, and after my internship I have not gotten the chance to start learning, but this semester seems like a right time.

SECONDARY RESEARCH

Research Question

  • How does a front-end framework assist me with developing my portfolio?

After having my research question, I set up specific terms which I used to conduct my secondary research, in order to answer my research question. Using mainly articles, YouTube video's and blogs, I noticed that I was not able to answer my research question, since all of my sources concluded the same thing: it all comes down to personal preference. This was an answer I could not do anything with, which meant that I had to dive deeper into research.

PRIMARY RESEARCH

Expert interview

By making use of the expert interview from the DOT-framework, I would be able to get clarifaction on whether a front-end framework would assist me with creating my portfolio. Since my semestercoach Bardt is also a front-end architect, he has plenty of experience working with front-end frameworks.

I prepared several questions, and during the expert interview, it has clarified that the use of a front-end framework for this project would not be of any added value, because of the limited time that was left, and that I should work with the knowledge that I already have.

CONCLUSION

Based on the results I gathered from this research, I can safely conclude that a framework will not be used for this portfolio project. Learning a framework has many advantages, for example for consistency in big projects.

However, there is little added value for using a framework in my portfolio. This is mainly due to the time constraints and its learning curve. I will however be working with a front-end framework in my personal project.
