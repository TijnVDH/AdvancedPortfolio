PERSONAL PROJECT

FINDING INTERNSHIP

DELIVERABLE CONTAINING THE PROCESS I TOOK TO FINDING MY GRADUATION INTERNSHIP.

INTRO

Since this is my seventh semester of my bachelor, my next semester will be my graduation semester. I have tried multiple sources which might have helped me find a graduation internship.

PROCESS

I started out by renewing my CV. Which can be seen here.

After this I reached out out to my connection where I found my previous internship. Unfortunately, there was no assignment he could come up with/forward me to.

After this, I knew I had to start looking at other possibilities, so I started looking on ASAM, from which I applied to some assignments, but nothing I was really excited about.

After realizing that I could do my graduation internship within the FHICT lectorate, I immediately took the chance and reached out to the contact person. He then forwarded me to Dennis (which was also our stakeholder).

Dennis had a list of assignments for me, from which two really stood out to me. Eventually I leaned towards the People's Peephole project more. This was because I was looking for a Media Design assignment, while still having Game Design aspects twisted in there. Because no other company offered something like this, I knew the lectorate was the right spot for me.

I reached out to the project owner Woody to arrange a meeting. In this meeting I received more information on what the project is about. Woody told me that the project is quite open, from which I created a project proposal basically from scratch.

Woody and Angela were both excited about what I was planning to do during my graduation period, and after some iterations the project proposal will (hopefully) be approved.
