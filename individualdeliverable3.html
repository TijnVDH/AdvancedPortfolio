INTRO

This page presents a summary of the project plan and its research questions. The project plan was the starting point of this project, and it documented the plan of approach for the project.

SCOPE & APPROACH

In the project approach, four main bulletpoints were tackled. This was done to create a clear overview on what and how things need to be done within this project. These points are as follows:

  • What are you going to do?
  • Why are you going to do it? (what do you want to learn and or achieve)
  • How are you going to do it?
  • What do you want to deliver in your first project?

RESEARCH QUESTIONS

For this project, I have a main reseqrch question:

  • Hoe presenteer ik mezelf als professioneel media designer in een helder en gestructureerd portfolio?

There are also multiple subquestions which have been answered in other deliverables in the portfolio. The subquestions in this project plan are as follows:

  • In which field is my ICT expertise?
  • How do I wish to present myself as a professional media designer?
  • How will I bring forward my expertise in my portfolio?
  • How might a front-end framework assist me in setting up my portfolio?
  • How am I going to create a user-friendly portfolio?
